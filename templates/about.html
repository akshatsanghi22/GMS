About Us

Discover how our Google Maps scraper can transform your business by providing accurate and valuable location data

Our Mission

Our Google Maps Scraper is designed to help businesses and researchers extract valuable data from Google Maps efficiently and accurately. We understand the challenges of manually collecting location data, which is why we've developed a powerful tool that automates this process.

Whether you're conducting market research, generating sales leads, analyzing competitors, or planning business expansion, our scraper provides the data you need to make informed decisions. With customizable search parameters and multiple export options, you can tailor the scraping process to meet your specific requirements.

We pride ourselves on delivering a user-friendly tool that doesn't require technical expertise to operate. Our intuitive interface allows anyone to extract Google Maps data quickly and easily, saving you time and resources.

Scraper Features

Business Information

Extract comprehensive business information including names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, business hours, and more directly from Google Maps listings.

Review Analysis

Gather customer reviews, ratings, and sentiment data to understand business reputation and customer satisfaction levels for your target locations.

Multiple Export Formats

Export your scraped data in various formats including CSV, JSON, and Excel, making it easy to integrate with your existing tools and workflows.

Customizable Searches

Define your search parameters, including location, keywords, radius, and result count to get precisely the data you need for your specific use case.

How It Works

  1. Define Your Search

    Enter your search query (e.g., "restaurants in New York") and specify the number of results you want to extract.

  2. Select Data Points

    Choose which data points you want to extract, such as business name, address, phone number, website, ratings, and reviews.

  3. Choose Output Format

    Select your preferred output format (CSV, JSON, or Excel) for the scraped data.

  4. Run the Scraper

    Click "Start Scraping" and our tool will extract the data from Google Maps based on your specifications.

  5. Download Results

    Once the scraping is complete, download your data and use it for your business needs.
