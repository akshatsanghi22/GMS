Business Data Extraction

Comprehensive Business Information

Our Google Maps scraper allows you to extract detailed business information for any location or category. This data is invaluable for market research, lead generation, and business development.

Data points include:

  • Business name and category
  • Full address and location coordinates
  • Contact information (phone, email, website)
  • Operating hours
  • Popular times data
  • Attributes and amenities
  • Photos and image URLs

This data can be exported in various formats (CSV, JSON, Excel) for easy integration with your existing tools and workflows.

Review Analysis

Understand Customer Sentiment

Customer reviews provide valuable insights into business reputation and customer satisfaction. Our scraper extracts detailed review data from Google Maps to help you understand what customers are saying.

Review data includes:

  • Overall rating and number of reviews
  • Individual review text and ratings
  • Reviewer information and dates
  • Review responses from business owners
  • Rating distribution

This data can help you identify trends, monitor customer satisfaction, and make data-driven improvements to your business.

Competitor Research

Gain Competitive Insights

Understanding your competitors is crucial for business success. Our Google Maps scraper helps you gather detailed information about competitors in your industry or location.

Competitive insights include:

  • Competitor location mapping and density
  • Service and product offerings
  • Customer ratings and review sentiment
  • Business hours and peak times
  • Special features and amenities

This competitive intelligence can inform your business strategy, help identify market gaps, and provide opportunities for differentiation.
